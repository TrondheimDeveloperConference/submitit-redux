Got something to say? Trondheim Developer Conference is the place to say it!

Trondheim Developer Conference takes place in Trondheim, Norway, on october 30, 2017. The conference offers a combination of technical talks and workshops in an informal atmosphere with an expected attendance of more than 600 developers.

Submitting a talk to Trondheim Developer Conference 2017

Submitting: Once you have submitted your talk you will receive a confirmation by email. In this email you'll find a link to a page where you can further update your submission should you wish.

Publication: The information given in this form will be published in the program, unless specifically stated otherwise.

Language: Please complete this form using the same language that you'll be presenting in. In other words, if you plan on presenting in Norwegian, skriv på norsk!

Deadline: The deadline for submissions is June 30 2016, but feel free to submit your talk early :-)

Format: All talks are in the 30 minute format
